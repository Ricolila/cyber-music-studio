Cyber Music Studio

when finished, Cyber Music Studio will be standalone in-browser app for recording and mixing music. But it's not finished yet, so now there are only these instruments:

  • Severák's ideal groovebox - groovebox created by cherrypicking ideas from other software.
  • ~ Regenschori - organ with tape recorder inspired by Sankei Stereo Entertainer
  • ~ Heartbeat GM - subset of your generic 90s General MIDI keyboard.
  • ~ KASIA - vowel-consonant synthesizer in style of 80s Casiotones
  • ~ FM synth - basic but usable FM synth (not finished yet)
  • ~ Sample propulsion laboratory - PCM sampler playground
  • Lyra 8 - a parody/homage to Soma Laboratory Lyra-8
  • traveller and analog nomad - port of my old VST plugins
  • AKWF playground - testing tool for Adventure Kid Waveforms collection

Those marked with ~ before name now supports WebMidiLink so you can use them in WebSequencer or ChrossPad. Note that only some features are supported (only channel 1 and no pitch bend yet etc).

For your sequencing needs you can try Alda web workspace.

Have a fun!
