vowel-consonant synthesis

In 1980s CASIO released series of inexpensive Casiotone keyboards. Some of them were using mysteriously named vowel-consonant synthesis.

It's description in US patent 4419919A sounds complicated but it's very simple in fact - it uses two square waves with different envelope filtered by static low pass filters.
